OUR PROCESS

Each participant company shares its responsibility data through a managed, supported process. Responsible 100 uses this to create benchmarks to help all businesses to improve.

1. DETERMINE YOUR PRIORITY ISSUES 

20 responsibility issues across 8 different categories are presented. 

Respondents are asked to indicate whether they consider the issue to be either:

>>  CRITICAL
>>  IMPORTANT
>>  A FACTOR
>>  NO IMPACT 

2. REFLECT ON YOUR CURRENT PERFORMANCE

We provide businesses with the opportunity to gain a better understanding of the issues they want to work on by watching video explainers. Then, Responsible 100 supports businesses to reflect on the things they do and provide complete, live, written disclosures and case studies. 
The company is then supported in objectively categorising how it currently performs as either:

>>  EXCELLENT
>>  GOOD
>>  OKAY
>>  POOR 

These initial, approximate prioritisations and scores can subsequently be changed up or down when additional details are submitted and when there are further comparisons to consider.

3. CREATE YOUR RESPONSIBILITY INDICATOR

Your first Responsibility Indicator is created within an hour, and then updated and improved on an ongoing basis. It immediately reveals the issues on which further effort and focus are needed most, e.g. where priority level is high but performance is poor. It enables businesses to map out their current performance and future plans. It underpins ongoing improvement.

4. JOIN EXPERT-LED WORKSHOPS

Responsible 100 partners with incredibly impressive and inspiring issue experts. The brilliant, passionate people we work with provide you with bespoke help and guidance.

Together, we'll help you to improve your performance on all your priority issues. Find out more here about of workshops with the experts.

5. NETWORK YOUR KNOWLEDGE & DATA

Responsible 100 businesses share details of their previous and current performance levels, and of where they strive to be in the future. They share their practices, knowledge and experience.

The stories that participating businesses provide are gathered together, with permission, into co-created benchmarks. These are the maps of the POOR, OKAY, GOOD and EXCELLENT of all the most important responsibility issues faced by business and wider society. The benchmarks become increasingly valuable with each new story that is added.

6. DEMONSTRATE OPENNESS & HONESTY

We believe that Responsible 100 constitutes the most rigorous test of responsibility that there is. Yet it is also the most doable, intuitive and supported test too.

All organisations participating in Responsible 100 are supported in mapping and communicating their performance on all the issues that matter most. 

They support other businesses in this process, and these other businesses support them in return. 

And when the time is right, we enable businesses to share these live, honest, frank accounts of themselves, their cultures and their policies and practices to all their stakeholders.
